Archway Partnership's

CyberArch Initiative

UGA's Archway Partnership is a nationally recognized Public Service and Outreach program addressing community-identified economic and development needs.

Archway's CyberArch program is addressing the challenges of cybersecurity for Georgia's communities and businesses.

Communities

Carl Vinson Institute of Government

Since 1927, the Carl Vinson Institute of Government has worked to promote excellence in government through education, research, and policy analysis to help public officials server Georgia communities.

The institute is collaborating with CyberArch to discover how we can help government leaders assess the risk to their communities, navigate training challenges, and meet the legal obligations of the information age.

Businesses

Small Business Development Center

UGA's Small Business Development Center provides training and resources to help grow small businesses in our state.

The cyber threat to small business is real and continuing to grow. When considering downtime, data recovery, and lost business, a cyber attack can be devastating.

Prepare your business and become #CyberStrong before an attack happens.
